Busalot

About us:

Busalot is a bus ticket comparison site and application that helps bus travelers to compare different bus companies prices on different routes so that they can choose which company and busses they would like to travel with. We let the companies register with us and add their Routes to the system. Busalot does not sell tickets it only shows the routes that busses travel and the rates and features the busses have. Busses can be filtered by price, rating, trailor space for travelers with a lot of bags, and also by disability requirements. Busalot wants to give travelers the cheapest and best bus for their travel needs. Busalot promises the best service which saves you time, money and it definetely puts you first.
